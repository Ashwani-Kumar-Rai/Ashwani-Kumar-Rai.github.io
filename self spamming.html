Litmus test for self spamming and shit talking

the best way to not end confusion or not reach to a goal is to take a sidestep . In life generally self spamming and shit talking are great sidesteps .

let's first define these two terms then we will talk about cure .

Self Spamming are :

--> Is asking questions to people , that you know don't have any answer , or they pretending to have an answer.(be carefull there are a lot of sales guys in every field , and thier job is to convince you ,"that they know the shit they are talking about") .

--> your question does not matter . eg: who will win this election

--> you are asking question ,that is not possible for any human to answer .

--> you don't know what exactly you are asking . "you don't have a definition for the keywords that you are using "


Shit talking are :

-->Whatever you are saying is meaningless .

--> prentending you know about something

--> if there is a good topic going on , and someone said something to derail the conversation .


In the above situations it does not matter where you get the answer from the other person or not .. you lost while asking the question .

CURE :

You shoukd have some idea about the output space of the answer .

think about it whenever you go to a shop and you pay money , you have some idea what you are about to get .

In self spamming and shit talking , you are paying in time and getting nothing . i am not saying don't enjoy and make jokes .

But when you are asking a serious question ,you should have some idea of the output space to validate ,what you just bought with your time.
